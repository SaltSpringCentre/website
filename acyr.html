A Tradition of Connection

For over fifty years, the Annual Community Yoga Retreat has brought together practitioners, families, and seekers from around the world. Immerse yourself in the warmth of community, deepen your yoga practice, and reconnect with your inner peace at the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga.

Rooted in the teachings of Baba Hari Dass, the retreat offers an authentic yoga experience that includes asana, pranayama, meditation, philosophy, kirtan, and sacred ceremony. Whether you are a lifelong practitioner or stepping onto the mat for the first time, the ACYR welcomes you home.

Work honestly, meditate every day, meet people without fear, and play.
Baba Hari Dass
Authentic Yoga Lineage

Deepen Your Practice

The retreat draws on a rich lineage of classical yoga, with teachings rooted in the tradition of Baba Hari Dass. Expect kirtan, sacred ceremonies, pranayama, philosophy, meditation, and asana classes led by experienced teachers.

From sunrise meditation to evening kirtan, every moment is an opportunity to grow and connect.

Fun for Everyone

Family Friendly

Classes are offered for all skill levels, from gentle yoga to advanced practice. The retreat is a welcoming space for families, with dedicated children's programming and activities for all ages.

Come as you are. Everyone is welcome.
