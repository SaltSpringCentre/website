About the Centre

Founded in 1981 by followers of Baba Hari Dass (1923-2018), a silent monk who communicated only through a small chalkboard for 66 years, the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga sits on 69 acres of organic gardens, orchards, food forest, wetlands, and temples.

Operated by the Dharma Sara Satsang Society, the Centre offers yoga retreats, teacher training, karma yoga, Ayurveda wellness, and community satsang. Revenue from the shop directly supports the Centre's programs, gardens, and facilities.
