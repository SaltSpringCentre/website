A Tradition of Connection

For over fifty years, the Annual Community Yoga Retreat has brought together practitioners, families, and seekers from around the world. Immerse yourself in the warmth of community, deepen your yoga practice, and reconnect with your inner peace.

Rooted in the teachings of Baba Hari Dass, the retreat offers an authentic yoga experience that includes asana, pranayama, meditation, philosophy, kirtan, and sacred ceremony.

"Work honestly, meditate every day, meet people without fear, and play."
Baba Hari Dass
Authentic Yoga Lineage

Deepen Your Practice

The retreat draws on a rich lineage of classical yoga. Expect kirtan, sacred ceremonies, pranayama, philosophy, meditation, and asana classes led by experienced teachers.

Fun for Everyone

Family Friendly
