Primary Venue

Program House & Satsang Room

The hub of Centre life and the primary facility for many popular programs. A heritage house featuring guest rooms, library, dining facilities, and the main yoga space.

  • 1,290 sq ft hardwood yoga and Satsang space with wood stove
  • Beautifully appointed library
  • Dining facilities and tea bar
  • Guest rooms
Intimate Space

Garden House & Yoga Studio

The perfect cozy venue for your next retreat or meeting, nestled between Centre gardens and temples.

  • 525 sq ft carpeted yoga space
  • 5 guest rooms accommodating up to 9 people
  • Free wifi, yoga props (mats, blankets, cushions, blocks)
  • Tea bar access
