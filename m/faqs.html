Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

General

How do I get to the Centre?

Salt Spring Island is accessible by BC Ferries from Tsawwassen, Swartz Bay, and Crofton. Harbour Air offers float plane service. The Centre is at 355 Blackburn Road, a 10-minute drive from Ganges village. See our Directions page for details.

Do I need a car?

No, but cars are welcome. The Centre is a 40-60 minute walk to Ganges, with bus service about 10-15 minutes away. Carpooling among participants is common.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 4:00 PM. Check-out is at 11:00 AM. Some programs have different times noted in your registration confirmation.

Is the Centre kid-friendly?

The Centre primarily offers adult programming. The Annual Community Yoga Retreat includes dedicated children's activities.

Can I bring my pet?

We are unable to accommodate pets.

Accommodation

What are the guest rooms like?

The rooms are cozy with comfortable beds and linens. Private rooms have 1 twin bed; shared rooms have 2-3 twin beds. All rooms have shared washrooms except one ensuite room.

What are the camping facilities like?

Available May through September in forest or meadow settings with hot outdoor showers and outhouses. Bring your own tent, sleeping bag, and bedding. No fires, stoves, or candles permitted.

What should I bring?

Personal toiletries, comfortable clothing, alarm clock, slippers, water bottle, travel mug. Hiking shoes for the 1.7km trail. Bathing suit for sauna sessions.

Will I have internet access?

Wireless internet is available in the main program building and Garden House. Laptops and phones are not permitted in dining areas.

Can I make phone calls?

Cell service is unreliable on property but available in Ganges. A courtesy phone in the dining room is available for emergencies and taxi calls.

Meals

What food is served?

The Centre has a reputation for high-quality, nutritious, well-balanced cuisine. The diet is lacto-vegetarian with gluten-free and vegan options available. Most produce comes from our organic farm.

I have a severe allergy. Can you accommodate me?

Our kitchen environment cannot guarantee complete allergen prevention. Please contact us directly before registering.

Can I bring my own food?

Personal non-perishable snacks are acceptable. Please do not store food in tents or bring alcohol to the property.

Yoga Practice

Is this suitable for beginners?

Yes. Most programs are suitable for beginners and those new to yoga. The Yoga Teacher Training requires a minimum of one year's experience.

Can I bring my own coffee?

As part of a yogic lifestyle, we minimize the use of stimulants like caffeine. Black tea is available.
