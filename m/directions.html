Getting Here

355 Blackburn Road, Salt Spring Island, BC V8K 2B8

By Ferry

From Vancouver (Tsawwassen)

BC Ferries to Long Harbour. Approximately 3 hours including transfer at Swartz Bay or direct sailing.

From Victoria (Swartz Bay)

BC Ferries to Fulford Harbour. Approximately 35 minutes.

From Duncan (Crofton)

BC Ferries to Vesuvius Bay. Approximately 20 minutes.

BC Ferries: 1-888-BC-FERRY | bcferries.com

By Air

Harbour Air: 1-800-665-0212 | harbourair.com

SeaAir: 1-800-447-3247

Getting Around

  • Silver Shadow Taxi: 250-537-3030 (cash only)
  • Salt Spring Car & Scooter Rentals: 250-537-3122
  • BC Transit serves ferry terminals and Ganges
  • The Centre does not offer shuttle service. The nearest bus stop is a 15-minute walk.
