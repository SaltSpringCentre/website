Concert Series

Music for Peace 2026

Music for Peace is a concert series hosted at the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga, featuring classical Indian music, kirtan, and world music performances. Concerts are held in the intimate Satsang Room, a beautiful space with natural acoustics and room for approximately 50 guests. All are welcome.

What to Expect

Classical Indian Music

Performances featuring traditional instruments including sitar, tabla, harmonium, violin, and mridangam.

Kirtan & Devotional Music

Community chanting and call-and-response singing in the bhakti yoga tradition.

Intimate Venue

The Satsang Room seats approximately 50 guests in a warm, acoustically rich space.

By Donation

Most concerts are offered by donation. Some special events may have a suggested contribution.

Upcoming Concerts

Concerts run monthly from March through November. Visit our calendar for specific dates, performers, and reservation details.

View Concert Schedule

Venue

All Music for Peace concerts take place in the Satsang Room at the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga, 355 Blackburn Road, Salt Spring Island. Doors typically open at 6:30 PM with concerts beginning at 7:00 PM. Light refreshments may be available.
