Yoga Intensive Program

The Yoga Intensive is a multi-day immersion designed for practitioners who want to deepen their understanding of classical yoga. Under the guidance of experienced teachers in the tradition of Baba Hari Dass, participants explore asana, pranayama, meditation, and yogic philosophy in an intimate ashram setting.

What to Expect

Asana Practice

Multiple daily sessions exploring alignment, breath, and awareness in classical postures.

Pranayama & Meditation

Guided breathwork and meditation practices rooted in Raja Yoga.

Yogic Philosophy

Study of the Yoga Sutras of Patanjali and practical application of yogic principles.

Sattvic Living

Vegetarian meals from the Centre's farm, daily rhythm aligned with nature, community practice.

Program Details

  • Location: Salt Spring Centre of Yoga, Salt Spring Island, BC
  • Duration: 5 days (typically July)
  • Includes accommodation, all meals, and program materials
  • Open to all levels of practitioners
  • Check calendar for upcoming dates
