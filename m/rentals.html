Program House

Satsang Room

The main ceremony and gathering space in the Program House. This warm, light-filled room has hosted decades of yoga programs, satsangs, kirtan, workshops, and community celebrations. With seating for up to 80 people, a full sound system, and a stage area, it serves as the heart of programming at the Centre.

The adjacent commercial kitchen and dining hall make the Program House ideal for multi-day retreats with catered meals.

Yoga & Accommodation

Garden House & Yoga Studio

A secondary yoga venue nestled among the gardens, the Garden House offers a tranquil practice space with natural light and views of the surrounding forest. The dedicated yoga studio comfortably holds 20 to 30 practitioners.

The building also includes accommodation rooms for retreat participants, making it a self-contained option for smaller groups seeking an intimate experience on the land.

Geodesic Venue

Pond Dome

A geodesic dome venue situated by the pond, offering a unique and inspiring setting for workshops, sound healing, meditation, and small gatherings. The circular architecture creates a natural sense of unity and containment.

Surrounded by water, trees, and birdsong, this space brings the outside in. Perfect for groups of up to 30 seeking something beyond a conventional room.

Wellness

Sauna

A traditional wood-burning sauna available as part of retreat packages or by special arrangement. After a day of practice, teaching, or deep work, the sauna offers a quiet place to release, restore, and reconnect with the body.

Set apart from the main buildings and surrounded by forest, the experience is meditative in its simplicity.

Accommodation

Private and shared rooms in the Program House and Garden House. Simple, clean, and peaceful.

Accommodation ranges from $1,870 (camping) to $5,390 (private en suite) for multi-week programs. Weekend retreats include accommodation in the package price.
