The School Building Needs a New Roof

The Salt Spring Centre School has served generations at the Centre, hosting classes and programs. The roof shingles have reached end-of-life and require replacement, with work scheduled for this summer.

Founded in 1983 following guidance from Baba Hari Dass, the school began with 12 students and grew into a learning space emphasizing peace, care for one another, and lifelong love of learning, guided by yoga and self-awareness practices.
