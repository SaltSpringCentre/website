We honour the life and legacy of [Lakshmi (Teresa)](https://saltspringcentre.com/founding-member-feature-lakshmi-mcphee/) McPhee, whose devotion and service helped shape the early foundation of the Satsang and Centre. From the early days of satsang and work parties, to tending gardens, holding children, organizing retreats, and supporting generations of seekers—Lakshmi lived the spirit of karma yoga. Her life was an offering.

A long-time student of Baba Hari Dass, Lakshmi met life—and her recent transition—with extraordinary clarity and grace. Grounded in the knowing that the soul is eternal, she crossed peacefully on January 2, surrounded by love.
