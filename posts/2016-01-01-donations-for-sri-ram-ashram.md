[![sri-ram-ashram](images/e41849fa_sri-ram-ashram.jpg)](http://sriramfoundation.org/)Sri Ram Ashram, (literally “home” in Hindi), is located in the northern state of Uttaranchal, on 16 acres of rural farmland near the town of Haridwar. The Ashram, founded in 1984, was inspired by Baba Hari Dass. To learn more about this wonderful home for abandoned and destitute children, visit their [website](http://sriramfoundation.org/).
Supporting Sri Ram Ashram is a way of showing our appreciation for the many gifts of teaching that Baba Hari Dass has given us.
