### Keeping the Flame Burning June 14 – 16, 2013

The Annual General Meeting of the [Dharma Sara Satsang Society](https://saltspringcentre.com/about/dharma-sara-satsang) will be held on June 15 during the Keeping The Flame Burning weekend: a gathering to celebrate Babaji’s teachings in a weekend of stories, practice and celebration.
More information will be posted on the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga website as it becomes available.
