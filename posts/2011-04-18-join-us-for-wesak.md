Join us close to the full moon in May for Wesak, a celebration of the birth, enlightenment and passing of the Buddha. It will be an evening of prayers, chanting, readings and meditation led by representatives of Buddhist groups on the island, including Tibetan, Zen and Vipassana.
**This is a FREE event. Everyone is welcome!**
Thursday, May 3rd, 2012
7:30pm – 9:30pm
Salt Spring Centre of Yoga
355 Blackburn Road
