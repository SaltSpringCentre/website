### Life at the Centre - September  2025

This September, the Centre was alive with devotion, celebration, and community connection. We gathered for Babaji’s Ārādhanā, a beautiful ceremony of remembrance for our beloved teacher. The month also brought us together for Hanuman Puja, Ganesh Puja, and vibrant Navaratri celebrations, where music and joyful dancing honoured the Divine Mother.
We were delighted to host the local Chamber of Commerce Mixer, opening our doors to the wider Salt Spring community. Out in the fields, the farm team’s dedication led by Paz shone brightly — our pumpkin won 3rd place at the Salt Spring Fall Fair, and our handmade sourdough and gluten-free scones made by Elise and team proudly took 2nd place! 🥖🎃
Behind the scenes, karma yogis kept the Centre humming with warmth and welcome, while the Heritage Program House received its full repaint thanks to your generous support — a beautiful renewal of one of the Centre’s most cherished gathering spaces. ✨
