*Here at the Salt Spring Center of Yoga, where people meet,*

*A peaceful place, where hearts feel sweet.*

*Laughter and kindness fill the air,*

*In this warm community, love and care.*

[ticon icon="heart" color="000" size="16px" margin\_right="" margin\_left="" margin\_top="" margin\_bottom="" link=""]

*On the farm, hands in the ground,*

*Every karma yogi brings joy all around.*

*West field or the garlic patch, they plant with care,*

*Seeds of joy, growing everywhere.*

[ticon icon="heart" color="000" size="16px" margin\_right="" margin\_left="" margin\_top="" margin\_bottom="" link=""]

*Deer roam softly around the place,*

*While cedar trees stand tall with easy grace.*

*And with some luck you may catch,*

*A glimpse of Prince the cat, wandering around the rainbow patch.*

[ticon icon="heart" color="000" size="16px" margin\_right="" margin\_left="" margin\_top="" margin\_bottom="" link=""]

*The main building holds tales of those who came,*

*With Babaji's heritage, a cherished name.*

*Did you hear the sound of the conch that calls everyone near?*

*Time to gather for a meal, a moment so dear.*

[ticon icon="heart" color="000" size="16px" margin\_right="" margin\_left="" margin\_top="" margin\_bottom="" link=""]

*Different smells fill the kitchen air,*

*Ganesh watches closely, as we cook with care*

*Dish service one, two, and three,*

*Together we serve, in harmony.*

[ticon icon="heart" color="000" size="16px" margin\_right="" margin\_left="" margin\_top="" margin\_bottom="" link=""]

*Who knew such simple things could be*

*The source of joy, so wild and free?*
