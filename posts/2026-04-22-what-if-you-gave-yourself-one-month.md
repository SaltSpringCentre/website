There is a particular kind of tired that rest doesn't fix.

You know the one.

Where even after a good night's sleep, a walk outside, a quiet morning, something still feels thin. Stretched. Like you've been giving and giving and haven't quite found your way back to yourself.

We see you. And we want to tell you about something.

This June 29, we open the doors of the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga to 15 people for a four-week [Yoga Study & Service Immersion](http://https://saltspringcentre.com/programs-retreats/yoga-study-and-service-immersion-program/) — one of the most quietly life-changing things we offer.

It is not a retreat where you arrive, receive, and leave. It is something richer than that.

You will rise early and breathe together. You will study the Yoga Sutras and the Bhagavad Gita, not as ancient texts but as maps for living. You will work with your hands in the kitchen, the garden, the land and discover that this, too, is yoga. You will share meals and laughter and stillness with people who are searching, just like you.

**Baba Hari Dass, whose teachings are the heart of this Centre, wrote:**

## *"My only aim is to bring people in sadhana. Sadhana includes developing positive qualities, building right conduct, closeness with your parents, friends, and society, right livelihood."*

That is what this month holds. Not a quick fix. A genuine practice. A way back to yourself. Only 15 spots. Applications are reviewed through a short interview process.

Applications open till June 15, 2026
