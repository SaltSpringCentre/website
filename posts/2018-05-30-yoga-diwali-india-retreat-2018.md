[Mount Madonna Center](https://www.mountmadonna.org/) & [Sri Ram Ashram](http://sriramfoundation.org/) invite you to join us as we practice Yoga and celebrate the light of India amid the joy of Sri Ram Ashram.

### **Oct 29 - Nov 10, 2018**

JOIN US where the Ganges River meets the Himalayas of Northern India. Experience the country’s most joyful festival season while deepening your practice of Yoga. Begin your adventure with a one-day cultural tour of Delhi. Then off to Haridwar where you will live and experience Sri Ram Ashram. Explore local towns and villages, while connecting with over sixty children who call the ashram home.
