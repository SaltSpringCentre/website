Following their highly successful Ayurveda & Yoga Retreat at the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga in June, Farah Nazarali and Muneera Wallace are hosting a second retreat at the Centre from [**November 11 - 13, 2022**](https://saltspringcentre.com/events/ayurveda-yoga-retreat-farah-and-muneera/).
What makes their retreats so popular? Both Farah and Muneera are highly-skilled and much-loved figures in the fields of yoga and Ayurveda and attract attendees eager to learn more.
