Peter Rowatt passed suddenly on Dec 31st. He and Nona have been involved with the Satsang since our first Yoga Retreat.

A few retreats later Babaji encouraged Peter to play his bagpipes for the wake up of the camp each morning. Always a great sense of fun and devoted to Nona's care these later years. Their great love of Nature has also supported the Centre's orchard for many years.

We hold Lakshmi and Peter's families and friends, and Satsang family, with all who knew and will miss them dearly, in our prayers and bow in gratitude for their lives with us. OMMMMM Peace.
