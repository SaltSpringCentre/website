### Life at the Centre - August 2025

This August, the Centre was full of life. We welcomed the Satsang back Home at the 51st Annual Community Yoga Retreat, celebrating family, yoga, and the timeless teachings of Baba Hari Dass. We honoured Master Yogi Baba Hari Dass (Babaji) and all spiritual teachers through an ancient Vedic ceremony (Yajña) during the Guru Purnima celebration.
We also hosted the Sacred Community Yoga Retreat, organized by Ram Dass devotees, a special gathering for this community to come together in a sacred and supportive space.
Behind the scenes, we tended the land, picked blueberries, and shared fresh produce at the farm stand, while also making sure our guests felt at ease, nourished, and enjoyed delicious meals. We also hosted a wonderful Yoga & Wellness Retreat and honored Ganesh Chaturthi.
Thanks to your generous donations, the Program House is getting ready for a fresh coat of paint, with pressure washing now underway. 💖
