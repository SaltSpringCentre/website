We are delighted to offer a new program [![news-sandra-sagarmurti-shotton](images/013324ab_news-sandra-sagarmurti-shotton.jpg)](https://saltspringcentre.com/2013/01/introduction-to-ayurvedic-yoga-therapy/news-sandra-sagarmurti-shotton/)in 2013 focused on how to bring the therapeutic benefits of yoga and ayurveda to individuals and groups in many different contexts, supporting health challenges and lifestyle changes. This program may be of particular interest to YTT 200 graduates who wish to expand their knowledge base and enhance their teaching skills. Join us and see if Yoga Therapy might provide a new direction in your yoga path. This exciting opportunity will be facilitated by Sandra Sagarmurti Shotton E-RYT 500, an Ayurvedic Yoga Therapist and founder of  [Island Yoga Vista](http://yogavistananaimo.com/about-2/instructors/your-guide/) in Nanaimo. Sandra has been practicing and teaching ayurvedic yoga therapy for 16 years and has a wealth of experience to offer.  [Find out more...](/retreats-programs/ayurvedic-yoga-therapy/)
