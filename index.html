A Rare and Sacred Place

The Salt Spring Centre of Yoga is a non-profit retreat centre dedicated to teaching the core principles of yoga: peace, resilience, and belonging. Founded by master yogi Baba Hari Dass, the Centre is the west coast of Canada's longest running spiritual and wellness retreat centre.

Whether you're here for a weekend retreat, a yoga immersion, or a full-time karma exchange, the Centre becomes the container you need for deep, challenging, and playful personal work.

"Work honestly, meditate every day, meet people without fear, and play."
Baba Hari Dass
The Land

69 Acres of Living Sanctuary

An organic farm, fruit orchard, permaculture food forest, pristine streams and ponds, and the Dandaka Forest, one of the most untouched cedar-swamp wetlands in North America.

Over 110 species call this land home. The Centre borders the Salt Spring Island Conservancy's Blackburn Lake nature reserve.

Our Founder

Baba Hari Dass

A master yogi and scholar who undertook a vow of silence in 1952 and never spoke another word. He communicated by means of a small chalkboard, conveying a great deal in the fewest possible words.

Babaji left his body on September 25, 2018. His wisdom lives on in the institutions he created, his writings, and all those he touched.
