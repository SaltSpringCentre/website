Policies & Governance

Policies & Governance

Community agreements, workplace policies, and governance structure.

Shift Coverage Policy
  • It is your responsibility to find coverage for your shifts.
  • Use the WhatsApp Shift Coverage Channel to trade or bargain shifts.
  • This is not Tash's responsibility.
Sick Policy
  1. Doctor's notice required for extended illness (3 days or more).
  2. Notify your Area Lead immediately.
  3. Announce on Shift Coverage WhatsApp Channel.
  4. If no response, contact Area Lead and let Maya know.
  5. Last resource: Operations Coordinator provides coverage.
Meal Opt-Out Policy
  • We assume everyone eats all 3 meals daily.
  • Inform Kitchen by 9 AM on the Meal Opt-Out WhatsApp Channel.
  • For extended time away, Tash notifies Kitchen.
Schedule Change Policy
  • Minimum 2 weeks notice required for all schedule changes.
  • Submit by email to scheduler@saltspringcentre.com
