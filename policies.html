Centre Guidelines

Centre Guidelines

The following are not permitted on the Centre premises:

  • Alcohol, smoking, cannabis, illegal or non-prescription drugs
  • Incense, fires, candles, sparklers, camp stoves (except kitchens and ceremonies)
  • Meat, poultry, seafood, eggs
  • Scented or perfumed products
  • Outside shoes indoors
  • Public nudity
  • Swearing

Quiet Hours: 9 PM to 8 AM

Cancellation & Fee Policies

Effective January 25, 2024. Full payment is required at the time of booking.

In-Person Programs & Personal Retreats
  • 1+ month before arrival: 15% processing fee, remainder refunded
  • Within 1 month but 48+ hours before: 30% processing fee, remainder refunded
  • Within 48 hours or during program: No refunds issued
  • Centre cancellation: Full refund provided
  • Donations are non-refundable
  • 15% change fee to transfer between programs, plus any cost differences
Yoga Study & Service Immersion Program
  • Full payment required 3 weeks before program start; payment plans available
  • First 7 days after start: Retain 20% deposit + 15% processing fee; refund remainder
  • After 7 days: No refunds issued
  • Centre cancellation: Full refund provided
Online Programs

Cancellation for online programs that provide recordings are not eligible for refunds or credits. Centre cancellation results in full refund.

Wellness Treatments
  • Book 24+ hours in advance recommended
  • Cancellations accepted up to 24 hours prior
  • Late cancellations or no-shows charged in full
Sauna Bookings
  • 48-hour advance notice required for booking and payment
  • Cancellations must occur 24+ hours prior; no refunds after this deadline
  • Fire ban cancellations receive full refund
Damages

Guests are liable for damages beyond normal wear and tear, including replacement costs and deep cleaning expenses.
