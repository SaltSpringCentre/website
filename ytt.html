Yoga Teacher Training

200-hour residential immersion in classical yoga on Salt Spring Island.

200-Hour Certification

Immersive Teacher Training

A two-segment residential program allowing integration time between sessions. Live-in intensive training at the Salt Spring Centre of Yoga, surrounded by 69 acres of forest, gardens, and sacred land.

No distractions. A supportive community environment. A transformational immersive experience beyond standard classroom learning.

Curriculum

The Eight Limbs

Classical Ashtanga yoga as outlined by Patanjali.

Philosophy & Sutras

Yoga philosophy and the Patanjali Yoga Sutras.

Pranayama & Meditation

Breath work and meditation techniques.

Asana Practice

Postures, alignment, sequencing, and teaching methodology.

Anatomy & Physiology

Understanding the body in the context of yoga practice.

Teaching Methodology

How to lead, sequence, cue, and hold space.

Faculty

15+ multi-generational faculty members with combined decades of teaching experience. Specialists in philosophy, anatomy, pranayama, and more. Several instructors reside on-site for ongoing student support.
