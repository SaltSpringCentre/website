Programs & Immersions

Retreats

Step away from the noise and into the stillness. Our retreat programs range from guided weekend immersions and seasonal spring cleanses to extended silent retreats and deeply personal self-directed stays. Each retreat unfolds on 69 acres of forest, gardens, and sacred space, held by decades of practice and community.

Whether you are seeking renewal, clarity, or a first step onto the path, there is a retreat here for you.

Certification Program

Yoga Teacher Training

Our 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training is a residential immersion grounded in the classical teachings of Ashtanga Yoga as transmitted by Baba Hari Dass. Over three intensive weeks on-site, you will study asana, pranayama, meditation, anatomy, philosophy, and the art of teaching with integrity.

This is not just a certification. It is a deep dive into self-study and community, designed to transform your practice and prepare you to share yoga with authenticity and heart.

Live-In Volunteer Program

Karma Yoga

Karma Yoga is the yoga of selfless service, and it is the heartbeat of this community. Our live-in volunteer program invites you to dedicate 25 to 30 hours per week to the daily work of the Centre: cooking, gardening, cleaning, building, and caring for the land and each other.

In return, you receive accommodation, three vegetarian meals a day, and full access to all yoga classes and community programs. Three seasonal cohorts run throughout the year, each lasting several months. It is a rare opportunity to live simply, practice deeply, and belong to something larger than yourself.

Weekly Practice

Yoga Classes

The Centre offers a full weekly schedule of asana, pranayama, and meditation classes open to all levels. Whether you are a beginner or a longtime practitioner, our teachers guide with warmth and attention rooted in the classical tradition.

Sunday Satsang brings the community together for chanting, meditation, and teachings. Regular kirtan sessions fill the hall with devotional music and shared voice. These gatherings are free and open to everyone.
