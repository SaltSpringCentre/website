Directions

Getting Here

From Vancouver

BC Ferries from Tsawwassen to Long Harbour (direct to Salt Spring) or connect via Swartz Bay on Vancouver Island.

From Victoria

BC Ferries from Swartz Bay to Fulford Harbour. A scenic 35-minute crossing through the Gulf Islands.

By Float Plane

Harbour Air and Saltspring Air offer float plane service from Vancouver Harbour directly to Ganges Harbour on Salt Spring Island.

From the Ferry

The Centre is a 15-minute drive from both Fulford Harbour and Long Harbour ferry terminals. Follow signs toward Fulford-Ganges Road, then turn onto Blackburn Road.

From Nanaimo

BC Ferries from Crofton to Vesuvius Bay. A quick 20-minute crossing from the Cowichan Valley.

Getting Around

Silver Shadow Taxi: 250-537-3030 (cash only, book in advance). Salt Spring Car and Scooter Rentals: 250-537-3122. BC Transit buses serve the island with a stop at Blackburn Road.
